Revolutionising disease prevention in chickens

Gallucin

Gallucin is the first product from ReVAMP Supplements Ltd. This combination of antimicrobial peptides has been specifically designed to prevent the most common bacterial diseases in chickens, inlcuding necrotic enteritis, Salmonella and E.coli infections.

The product aims to reduce the need for antibioitcs by provideing an alternative isease prevention and growth enhancement plan. Simply adding Gallucin to chicken feed will reduce the incidence of disease and improve feed conversion.
